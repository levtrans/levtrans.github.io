FREQUENTLY

🚛 Moving Questions
How far in advance should I book my move? +
We recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance, especially on weekends and end-of-month dates which fill up fast. That said, we often accommodate same-day and next-day moves when availability allows — just call us!
Do you charge by the hour or offer flat rates? +
We offer both hourly and flat-rate pricing depending on your move size and destination. Hourly moves have a 2-hour minimum. Flat rates work great for long-distance and larger moves. We'll recommend the best option for your situation.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve all of greater Los Angeles — Van Nuys, Burbank, Glendale, Sherman Oaks, Encino, Northridge, Santa Monica, Pasadena, Long Beach, and beyond. We also do long-distance moves throughout California and across the US.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes. LEVTRANS is fully licensed by the California Public Utilities Commission (CALPUC) and carries full liability and cargo insurance. Your belongings are protected from the moment we pick them up to the moment we set them down.
What if something is damaged during the move? +
While damage is rare, we take full responsibility for any item damaged due to our crew's handling. We carry insurance and have a straightforward claims process. We'll make it right — that's our guarantee.
📦 Packing & Services
Do you provide packing materials? +
Yes! We provide all packing materials including boxes in various sizes, packing tape, bubble wrap, packing paper, and furniture blankets. Materials can be included in your quote or available as an add-on. We've got everything you need.
Can you move specialty items like pianos or safes? +
Absolutely. Our crew is trained and equipped to handle pianos, pool tables, gun safes, large appliances, antiques, and other specialty items. We use proper dollies, straps, and protective materials for every specialty piece.
Do you offer storage if my new place isn't ready? +
Yes. We can coordinate secure, climate-controlled storage solutions to hold your belongings for days, weeks, or months until you're ready for delivery. Ask us about storage options when you get your quote.
Will you disassemble and reassemble furniture? +
Yes — basic furniture disassembly and reassembly is included with most moves at no extra charge. Beds, bed frames, desks, and similar pieces are no problem. Complex items like modular shelving may incur a small additional fee.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Cancellations made 48+ hours before the move date receive a full refund of any deposit. Cancellations within 24 hours may forfeit a one-hour fee. Life happens — call us and we'll always try to work something out that's fair for everyone.
